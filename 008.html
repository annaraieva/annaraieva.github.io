Pumpkin pancakes with melted butter

☺ Hanna Raieva | Amerikan food | Pancakes |

Rate it first

Preparation time:
2 hours 30 minutes		 Servings:
4		 Difficulty:
Moderate

Ingredients:

  • 400 g of peeled pumpkin (200 g of puree)
  • 200 ml of 2.5% milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 g of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 50 g of butter (+10 g for drizzling)
  • 10 g of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of oil for greasing the pan
